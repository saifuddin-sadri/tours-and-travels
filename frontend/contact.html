❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour package? +

Simply fill out the inquiry form on this page or WhatsApp us. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your booking, discuss customizations, and share payment details.

Can I customize a package? +

Absolutely! All our packages are fully customizable — you can change the duration, hotel category, add/remove activities, or combine multiple destinations. Just let us know your preferences.

What is the cancellation policy? +

We offer flexible cancellations. Cancel 30+ days before departure for a full refund (minus processing fee). 15-30 days: 75% refund. Less than 15 days: 50% refund. Discuss with our team for special cases.

Are flights included in the packages? +

Most packages cover ground transportation, hotels, and guided tours. Flights are typically excluded but can be arranged at the best rates through our flight booking partners. Ask us for a combined quote!

Do you offer group discounts? +

Yes! Groups of 6+ people get special discounts starting at 8%. For corporate groups of 15+, we offer fully customized packages with dedicated managers. Contact us for a group quote.
